Frequently Asked Questions

  • A Social Set includes one of each profile we support: Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Pinterest, LinkedIn, YouTube, Threads and Snapchat. It helps you organize your content by brand or client.

  • Access Groups make it easy to manage who can access media libraries, social profiles, and calendars within Later Social. They are especially helpful for teams and agencies working across multiple brands or clients.

  • Yes! Auto Publish works for Instagram (business + creator accounts), Facebook, TikTok (business account), LinkedIn, Pinterest, YouTube, Threads and Snapchat. We offer notification publishing for Instagram and TikTok. If you choose this option you'll get a push notification when it's time to post. Tap the alert, and your content will open in Instagram or TikTok so you can publish it manually and add things like music and stickers.

  • Your post count is the number of scheduled posts you can create per month per social profile — not per Social Set. That includes all types of content, like Reels, Stories, and carousels. Posts count when they’re scheduled — not when they’re published.

    For example, on the Starter plan, you get 30 posts per profile per month. If you have 1 Instagram and 1 TikTok account, that’s 30 posts for each — 60 total.

  • All Later plans come with access to powerful analytics tools. The only difference? How far back you can look.

    • Starter: View performance data from the last 3 months to spot quick wins and track recent results
    • Growth: Access up to 1 year of data to uncover trends, measure long-term performance, and plan ahead
    • Scale: Up to 2 years of data and custom analytics with flexible filters, cross-platform views, and shareable reports

  • Future insights helps you stay ahead of the curve by showing you what’s about to trend in your industry. Powered by social listening, it analyzes emerging conversations and hashtags so you can create content that’s timely, relevant, and more likely to perform.

    Available on the Scale plan.

  • Competitive benchmarking helps you see how your Instagram strategy stacks up. Build custom competitor lists and track follower growth, engagement, and posting trends — so you can uncover gaps, find opportunities, and stay one step ahead.

    Available on the Scale plan.

  • Later Link in Bio is a mini website, built right into your Instagram or TikTok profile, that helps turn followers into customers. You can link to products, blog posts, videos, or any content you want - all from one link in your bio.

    Create a page that matches your brand, schedule posts that automatically update your Link in Bio, and track clicks to see what's working.

  • Later is perfect for managing social profiles with other people, whether it's having managers approving content, team members publishing posts, or contributors submitting media. Each plan comes with a set number of users. You can add additional users to each paid plan for $5 per additional user. Note: Starter plans are limited to 1 user.

  • Save 25% when you choose an annual subscription instead of a monthly subscription. Annual plans are billed once per year and renewed automatically.

  • Yes, qualified non-profit organizations can receive 50% off our annual Growth plan. Learn more.
